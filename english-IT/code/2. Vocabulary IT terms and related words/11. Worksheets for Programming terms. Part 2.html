What to do here?

First, scroll down this page until you see Resources for This Lecture.

Then, click to download the practice worksheet and do the exercises to revise vocabulary from the previous lecture.

Next, download the answer sheet and check your answers.

To memorise words better download the wordlist and write the translations in your own language.


Note. Printing out the worksheets may be more convenient, but it is not a must. You may open the worksheet on your computer and write your answers on a sheet of paper.


If you are not sure why your answer is incorrect or have any other questions about exercises, feel free to ask us in Q&A.


How to ask a question?

Click the button at the bottom of this page. First, check if someone else has already asked a similar question by entering some key words. If you get no results, then click the red button and type your question.
